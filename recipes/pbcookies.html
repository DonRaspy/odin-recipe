Peanut Butter Cookies

3 Ingredient Cookies

Follow this recipe to make very easy to make peanut butter cookies that will taste great!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 egg

steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix peanut butter, sugar, and one egg together in a bowl using an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Roll mixture into small balls and arrange on baking sheet; flatten each with fork, making criss-cross pattern.
  3. Bake in the preheated over for 10 minutes. Cook cookies on baking sheet before moving to plate.
