Philly Cheese Steak

This recipe will give your family a taste of Philadelphia with these easy to make philly cheese steaks.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh steak
  • 1 large yellow onion, sliced thin
  • 3 tablespoons Kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade & sauce.
  • 4 soft sandwich rolls
  • 1 (8oz) jar cheese spread

Steps

  1. Add oil to a non stick skillet
  2. saute oninos and 2 tablespoons of Teriyaki sauce until the onions are soft
  3. add the sliced steak and cook until meat browns slightly, add remaining Teriyaki sauce and combine
  4. place cheese on meat and stir until melted, mixing meat, cheese, and onions together
  5. scoop meat mixture onto sandwich rolls
